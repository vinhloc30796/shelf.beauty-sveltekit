# Design

## Foundation

Shelf Beauty Studio uses a cozy boutique brand system grounded in existing assets: rust brown `#8B3C22`, warm cream `#EAE1C9`, turquoise `#51BCBF`, the butterfly logo, and real studio photography.

## Color

- Background: warm studio cream and soft surface tints from the Shelf palette.
- Ink: deep brown-black for body text and headings.
- Primary: Shelf rust for logo, primary actions, active states, and key accents.
- Secondary: Shelf cream for quiet panels and practical information.
- Accent: turquoise used sparingly for status, focus, and small supporting details.
- Dark mode: deep brown surfaces with cream text and rust/turquoise accents.

## Typography

Use the existing sans-serif stack with stronger hierarchy, better line length, and balanced headings. Headings should feel confident but not oversized. Body copy stays direct, warm, and readable.

## Layout

The homepage is image-led with a strong first viewport, compact trust information, and two clear actions. Reviews use varied editorial blocks rather than uniform card grids. Contact is practical, with address, directions, map, and social links grouped for fast scanning.

## Components

Buttons use solid brand color, clear labels, visible focus rings, and comfortable mobile targets. Cards use modest radius and light separation, not decorative shadows. Navigation uses text labels without emoji.

## Motion

Use subtle hover and focus transitions only. Respect reduced motion by disabling nonessential transitions.
