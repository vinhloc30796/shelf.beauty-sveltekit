# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Shelf Beauty Studio serves two overlapping groups: Đà Lạt visitors who need fast confidence before booking, and local clients who want an easy way to return. Most visitors arrive on mobile, compare trust signals quickly, and need clear directions, hours, and booking links.

## Product Purpose

The site introduces Shelf as a warm, meticulous beauty studio in Đà Lạt, then helps visitors book an appointment or find the studio with minimal friction. Success means guests understand the atmosphere, trust the service quality, and can act from any route.

## Brand Personality

Warm, careful, and intimate. Shelf should feel like a real boutique studio with attentive hands, lived-in details, and a welcoming local rhythm, not a generic salon template.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic pink-beige salon layouts, stock-feeling polish imagery, emoji-heavy navigation, cold luxury minimalism, and card grids that make the studio feel interchangeable.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the room, the hands, and the atmosphere before abstract claims.
- Make booking and directions visible without making the page feel transactional.
- Use bilingual support where it helps visitors decide, not as duplicated clutter.
- Preserve the butterfly mark, rust brown, cream, and turquoise as Shelf identity anchors.
- Keep every surface readable, calm, and usable on mobile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, focus visibility, semantic structure, reduced motion, and touch targets. Vietnamese is primary, with English support in key decision areas for travelers.
